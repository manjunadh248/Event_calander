COURSES WE OFFER

LPU is a private university in India that offers various courses in different disciplines. Some of the courses offered by LPU

Engineering

LPU offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and PhD programs in various branches of engineering, such as computer science, electronics, mechanical, civil, biotechnology, etc. LPU also offers integrated and dual degree programs in engineering

Management

LPU offers BBA, MBA, and PhD programs in various domains of management, such as finance, marketing, human resource, international business, etc. LPU also offers integrated and dual degree programs in management

LAW

LPU offers BA LLB, BBA LLB, B.Com LLB, LLB, LLM, and PhD programs in law. LPU also offers integrated and dual degree programs in law

our facilities

LPU boasts the largest single-campus university in India, sprawling across 600 acres. The campus is a vibrant hub of learning, creativity, and cultural diversity.

libray

LPU’s library system caters to the academic community with more than 24+ lakh books and e-books across various subject fields.LPU has 11 branches of libraries across the campus. The largest one is the Central Library.

playground

The stadium houses 20 badminton courts, providing ample space for enthusiasts to play and compete.For those who love the fast-paced game of squash, LPU offers dedicated squash courts within the indoor complex.Sharpen your shooting skills at the well-equipped shooting range, where precision and focus are key.

hostles service

LPU offers a range of accommodation options, including single-seater rooms, multiple-seater rooms, and apartments. Students can choose between AC and non-AC rooms based on their preferences.The hostels have dining halls that serve four healthy and hygienic meals. Students can enjoy nutritious food without leaving the campus.
